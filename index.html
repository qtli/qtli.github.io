Biography

Hi, I am currently a graduate student at the Computer Science and Technology Department of Shandong University where I am a member of the information retrieval group. Before that, I obtained my B.E. degree in the Software Engineering Department at Shandong University. My research goal is to build machines with strong reasoning capabilities to assist information gathering and text generation through natural language interactions.

To this end, I have been working on natural language processing (NLP) techniques for e-commerce websites and dialogue systems — the two scenarios focusing on the single user and multiple users, respectively. Given the user-generated texts, various capabilities are required for generating user-satisfied texts, such as (1) identifying salient semantics, (2) sentiment analysis and interactions, and (3) commonsense reasoning.
